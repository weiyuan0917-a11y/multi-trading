# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Multi-Trading to be a practical, respectful, and safety-minded project.
Participants are expected to communicate with patience and professionalism,
especially when discussing risk, money, automation, and mistakes.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still asking for evidence and tests.
- Keep discussions focused on reproducible behavior, safety, and maintainable
  design.
- Avoid sharing private credentials, account information, or personal financial
  details in public channels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Encouraging unsafe trading practices without risk disclosure.
- Posting secrets, private account data, or exploit details publicly.
- Misrepresenting affiliation with Multi-Trading or upstream projects.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that violate
this code. Repeated or severe violations may result in restricted participation.
