Place semi-manual payment QR images here:

- `wechat-qr.jpg` or `wechat-qr.png`
- `alipay-qr.jpg` or `alipay-qr.png`
- `wise-qr.jpg` or `wise-qr.png`

Alternatively, configure `NEXT_PUBLIC_PAYMENT_QR_WECHAT_URL` and
`NEXT_PUBLIC_PAYMENT_QR_ALIPAY_URL` and `NEXT_PUBLIC_PAYMENT_QR_WISE_URL`
in `frontend/.env.local`.
